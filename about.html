Meet the girl behind the camera!

Hey friends!! I’m Talia Olson, a 21 year-old photographer based in beautiful Utah! I’m currently studying Business at Brigham Young University and recently returned from serving an LDS mission in the Colorado Denver North area. Photography is my passion, and I truly love capturing the joy, connection, and happiness in life — whether it’s you, your family, or your most meaningful moments. My goal is to create images that feel real and full of life. I’d love to meet you and help tell your story through my lens! Feel free to reach out with any questions or to book a session. Let’s make something beautiful together!

I specialize in natural light photography and love capturing real moments. Below is a fun list of my services:

    • Family Portraits

  2. Event Coverage
  3. Branding & Product Photography
